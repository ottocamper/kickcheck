KickCheck Methodology

KickCheck conducts comprehensive, independent verification of Kickstarter campaigns to provide backers with transparent, objective assessments. Our detailed inspection process evaluates multiple critical factors:

1. Business Identity Verification

We verify the legal existence and legitimacy of the company behind each campaign:

  • Corporate registration and business entity validation
  • Business address and contact information verification
  • Tax identification and legal status confirmation
  • Ownership structure and key personnel identification

2. Track Record Analysis

We review the creator's history of campaign delivery and business operations:

  • Previous Kickstarter campaign performance analysis
  • Delivery timeline accuracy and fulfillment rates
  • Backer feedback and complaint resolution history
  • Business continuity and operational stability
  • Product quality and customer satisfaction metrics

3. Manufacturing & Production Assessment

We evaluate the feasibility and readiness of production plans:

  • Manufacturing partner verification and capability assessment
  • Production timeline realism and risk analysis
  • Supply chain stability and material sourcing
  • Quality control processes and testing protocols
  • Scalability and capacity planning

4. Financial Readiness Review

We assess the financial planning and funding adequacy:

  • Funding goal accuracy and cost breakdown analysis
  • Financial projections and budget planning
  • Cash flow management and contingency planning
  • Payment processing and fulfillment cost estimates
  • Financial stability and resource availability

5. Communication & Transparency Evaluation

We review how well the creator communicates with backers:

  • Update frequency and quality of campaign communications
  • Transparency about risks and challenges
  • Responsiveness to backer questions and concerns
  • Honesty about delays and production issues
  • Post-campaign support and warranty policies

Scoring System

Each category is scored on a 25-point scale, with the total maximum score being 100 points. The final grade is assigned as follows:

  • A (90-100): Excellent - Very high confidence in delivery
  • B (80-89): Good - High confidence with minor concerns
  • C (70-79): Fair - Moderate confidence, some risks present
  • D (60-69): Poor - Low confidence, significant concerns
  • F (Below 60): Very Poor - Very low confidence, major red flags

Independence & Objectivity

KickCheck operates independently and does not accept payment from creators to influence grades. Our verification process is objective, data-driven, and based solely on verifiable facts and analysis. We maintain strict confidentiality of proprietary business information while providing transparent assessments to backers.

Report Validity

Reports are valid for the duration of the campaign. If significant changes occur (such as major delays, funding issues, or business changes), the report may be updated or re-evaluated. Backers are encouraged to check for updated reports if campaigns extend significantly beyond their original timeline.
